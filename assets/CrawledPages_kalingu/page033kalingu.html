Who is it for
All applicants must be at least 18 years old by the beginning of the program.

Anyone with an interest in the technologies and trends shaping our world are encouraged to apply. The program is not focused specifically on engineering or coding, and there is no requirement to have any prior technical experience in this area. Students are eligible to receive 4 U.S. university credits validated by the University of Montana. Contact our Admissions Team to find out more.
