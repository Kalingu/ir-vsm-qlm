Articles by Lawrence Bonk

  • Valve rolls out a new Steam store menu to make it easier to find games

    Valve has rolled out a new Steam store menu after testing it since July. This redesign makes it easier to find stuff you want to play.

  • Bowers & Wilkins' Px8 S2 headphones have updated drivers, improved ANC and a new look

    Bowers & Wilkins just released the Px8 S2 wireless headphones, a follow-up to the original Px8 unit. These headphones include a five-band customizable EQ and more microphones for ANC.

  • It's the last chance to get three free months of the Apple Music Family Plan

    Apple Music has a good deal for new subscribers. The Family Plan is free for three months if you don't already pay for a tier.

  • Spotify now directly integrates with DJ software

    Spotify just announced integration with popular DJ software platforms. This will allow users to create DJ sets by dipping into their playlists.

  • Costco reportedly stops selling Xbox consoles online

    Costco has reportedly stopped selling Xbox consoles online throughout the US and UK. It's still selling Nintendo and Sony products.

  • Riot's 2XKO fighting game hits early access on October 7

    Riot's long-awaited fighting game 2XKO will be available as an early access title on October 7. It's set in the League of Legends universe.

  • Meta to launch national super PAC against AI regulation

    Meta is launching a national super PAC that will be committed to fighting AI regulation. It has already been testing these waters with a PAC in California.

  • TikTok is tagging videos from Gaza with product recommendations

    It looks like TikTok has been tagging videos from war-torn Gaza with product recommendations. This is thanks to a new AI tool.

  • SpaceX's lunar lander could be 'years late' for a planned 2027 mission to the moon

    SpaceX's lunar lander, the Starship Human Landing System, could be delayed for years. This puts a manned lunar mission planned for 2027 in doubt.

  • Microsoft’s Gaming Copilot AI assistant is coming to Windows PCs and the Xbox mobile app

    Microsoft's long-gestating Gaming Copilot AI assistant is coming to more PC users and the Xbox mobile app in the near future. This toolset is sort of like an AI version of those old Nintendo help lines.

  • A former Facebook lobbyist is now in charge of the EU's Facebook regulator

    A former tech lobbyist has been named the head of the Data Protection Commission, which is a regulatory body in the EU. Niamh Sweeney worked at Meta for six years.

  • Google Discover is going to start showing social media posts and YouTube Shorts

    Google's Discover content feed is getting some big updates. You'll be able to follow particular creators and take a look at social media posts.

  • Garmin's new smartwatch for kids costs more than the Apple Watch SE

    Garmin's new Bounce smartwatch for kids costs $300 and the original was just $150. There are some new features.

  • The Apple Sports app finally adds widgets for tracking scores and schedules

    Apple is adding widgets to its free Apple Sports app. These will display scores and upcoming matches.

  • Viking survival sim Valheim will finally come to the PS5 in 2026

    The hit survival sim Valheim will be released for the PS5 in 2026. It's been on Steam since 2021.

  • YouTube just announced a bunch of AI tools for creators

    YouTube has revealed a whole boatload of tools for creators. Many of these involve AI in some capacity.

  • LG expands its line of xboom earbuds with two new models

    LG just announced two new earbud models in its xboom line. Both versions were tuned by musician will.i.am.

  • Apple's Mac mini M4 is up to $110 off right now

    The latest Mac mini is on sale via Amazon, with discounts up to $110 off the retail price. The base model is down to $499.

  • Amazon may be announcing new Echo and Kindle devices on September 30

    Amazon is holding a live press event on September 30 in NYC. It's likely the company will announce new devices, like an updated Echo speaker.

  • Balatro's big 2025 update won't be coming out this year after all

    Developer LocalThunk just revealed that the Balatro 1.1 update will not be coming in 2025. It will, however, be free on all platforms.
