Latest

  • Samsung Galaxy S25 FE review: Iterative to a fault

    With few upgrades and a price point that doesn't make much sense in the context of Samsung's lineup, the Galaxy S25 FE is hard to recommend at full price.

  • Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold review: The tankiest foldable

    While the Pixel 10 Pro Fold might not be as thin as the Galaxy Z Fold 7, the addition of a proper IP68 rating could be a phone saver while adding some needed durability to modern foldables.

  • iPhone Air review: Thinness with purpose

    It might not be the most affordable iPhone or the one with the most cameras, but for anyone who puts a premium on style and sleekness, the iPhone Air is without a doubt Apple's coolest handset in years.

  • iPhone 17 Pro and Pro Max review: An impactful redesign

    It’s a more substantive update than meets the eye.

  • iPhone 17 review: Closer to Pro

    The “regular” iPhone got a big refresh this year with the addition of a ProMotion display and Center Stage camera.

  • Google Pixel 10 review: The new smartphone standard

    The Pixel 10 blurs the line further between the base and pro models of Google’s phone series. With a 5x telephoto camera and most of the company’s most powerful AI features, it delivers a lot for a few hundred dollars less than its Pro sibling.

  • Google Pixel 10 Pro and Pro XL review: Redefining the smart in smartphone

    For those who appreciate convenience and usability over simple raw performance, the Pixel 10 Pro and Pro XL are the smartest choices among today’s Android flagship phones.

  • Nothing Phone 3 review: Not quite a flagship

    Nothing's first 'true flagship' has more cameras, more screen and a pixellated new feature in the Glyph Matrix.

  • Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 review: More screen, more battery but something's missing

    The Z Flip 7 has decent battery life, bigger screens and more AI smarts. However, the cameras are dated and Samsung could make more of the foldable’s second screen.

  • Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 7 review: Foldable phone nirvana (for a price)

    Thanks to a major redesign for 2025, Samsung may have reached foldable phone nirvana with the new Galaxy Z Fold 7.

  • Samsung Galaxy S25 Edge review: More than just super thin

    While thinness of the S25 Edge may be its biggest draw, the real prize is the way Samsung figured out how to make a much cooler version of the S25+.

  • Light Phone III review: Minimalism stretched to the point of frustration

    The Light Phone III is a well-designed device that can help you break your smartphone addiction — as long as you can accept the compromises.

  • Google Pixel 9a review: Basic in just the right way

    While it isn't super flashy, the Google Pixel 9a is a really good basic smartphone in all the right ways.

  • Nothing Phone 3a and 3a Pro review: Rising above the boring competition

    The Nothing Phone 3a and 3a Pro offer a great display, fun designs and, in the case of the 3a Pro, a tremendous camera for the price.

  • iPhone 16e review: What's your acceptable compromise?

    Our review of the iPhone 16e has it all: Real-world impressions, camera samples and comparisons to the competition.

  • OnePlus 13 review: A focused flagship that ignores the AI hype

    For anyone looking for a powerful new flagship smartphone, the OnePlus 13 is has a blazing new chip, a slick design and a massive battery, all for $100 less than an base Galaxy S25+.

  • Samsung Galaxy S25 review: More powerful, but more of the same

    The Samsung Galaxy S25 has useful AI features and an impressive battery life, but the samey hardware and unchanged price tag make for an unconvincing upgrade.

  • Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra review: Too much AI, not enough Ultra

    For the Galaxy S25 Ultra, Samsung added a new ultra-wide camera sensor, Gorilla Armor 2 and a big revamp to its AI suite. But is that enough to convince you to buy a new $1,300 phone?

  • Apple iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus review: Closing the gap to the Pro

    For once, you don’t have to wait a year to get the best new features from the iPhone 16 Pro. The iPhone 16 offers all the ones you will appreciate most, and for $200 less.
