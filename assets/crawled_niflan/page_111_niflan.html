Amy Skorheim

Senior Reporter, Buying Advice
Amy Skorheim has an English degree from UCLA and has been a commerce writer for six years, covering tech, men’s gear and outdoor paraphernalia. Her work has appeared in publications that include Futurism, Digg and Huckberry. After four years as the managing editor of GearMoose, she joined the commerce team at Engadget in 2022. She writes how-tos on Apple products, finds deals on tech and tests out small electronics for Engadget buying guides, creating far too many spreadsheets in the process. For five years, she lived off-grid in New Mexico and has wrangled more rattlesnakes than the average person.
